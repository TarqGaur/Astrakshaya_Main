Observational Astronomy

Observatory Setup &
Sky Survey Initiative

Astrakshaya is developing an independent ground-based observatory facility aimed at continuous sky surveys, celestial body tracking, and supporting launch trajectory validations. The setup integrates motorised equatorial mounts, high-resolution imaging sensors, and custom data pipelines for automated object cataloguing.

  • Automated tracking of near-Earth objects and satellite passages
  • Long-exposure deep-sky imaging for atmospheric calibration data
  • Integration with global astronomical databases for cross-referencing
  • Night sky light pollution mapping across Karnataka region

02
Atmospheric Platforms

HAPS — High Altitude
Pseudo-Satellite Plane

Our HAPS research focuses on designing a solar-electric unmanned aircraft capable of sustained flight at stratospheric altitudes (18–25 km). Positioned above commercial air traffic and below orbital satellites, the platform serves as a persistent relay node for communications, earth observation, and atmospheric sensing — at a fraction of satellite cost.

  • Ultra-lightweight airframe design with extended wingspan for lift efficiency
  • Solar cell array integration for continuous daylight power generation
  • Autonomous flight control and station-keeping at high altitude winds
  • Payload bay for remote sensing instruments and relay transponders
  • Stratospheric weather monitoring and data relay missions
Active 2024 —
03
Machine Intelligence

Aerospace AI — Vision
& Detection Models

The Astrakshaya AI research team has trained a suite of specialized vision models for aerospace applications — spanning real-world object detection from aerial platforms and satellite-grade earth observation analysis. These models are purpose-built for environments where speed, reliability, and low false-positive rates are mission-critical.

  • Real-world detection models: object recognition from drone and aerial feeds
  • Earth observation models: land use classification, vegetation indexing, anomaly detection
  • Custom training pipelines for domain-specific aerospace datasets
  • Onboard inference optimisation for edge deployment on HAPS payloads
  • Terrain and cloud segmentation for launch-window weather assessment
